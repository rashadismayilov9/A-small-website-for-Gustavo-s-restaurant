About us

Gustavo Martinez is the heart and soul behind Martinez Steakhouse. Born and raised in Argentina, he grew up surrounded by the rich tradition of open-fire grilling, known as asado. After years of traveling through Europe and learning classical culinary techniques, Gustavo combined his heritage with modern flavors to create a unique steakhouse experience. At his restaurant, every steak is hand-selected and grilled over natural flames, just the way Gustavo learned from his grandfather. Guests often say that what makes the experience unforgettable is not only the quality of the meat, but also Gustavo’s warm personality and passion for hospitality. Today, Martinez Steakhouse has become a beloved destination where tradition meets innovation on every plate.

Martinez Steakhouse is not a massive hall, but rather a cozy and elegant place where every guest feels at home. Gustavo designed his restaurant with just about 25 tables, enough to welcome food lovers while keeping the warm, personal atmosphere he cares so much about. Each table is arranged with attention to detail, from the lighting to the handcrafted wooden chairs, so guests can truly relax and enjoy their meal. For Gustavo, fewer tables mean more time and attention for each visitor — and that is what makes Martinez Steakhouse special.
